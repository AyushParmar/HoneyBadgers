THE LEGEND

It was a day after the Great Flippening that the signal was first received. The small packet of data came from a wallet untouched since the First Fork, beyond memories, but frozen in time on the blockchain.

Since then, a string of cryptic messages started arriving from Base 721, the HoneyBadger settlement in the world above which was lost centuries ago in the aftermath of the Second Fork War.

The decrypted message was simple, yet terrifying -- re-capture Base 721 --accompanied by a 24-digit seed phrase.

With their thick skin and super-sized attitude, the Honey Badgers exist peacefully in SoliCity, foraging for food, altcoins and airdrops. Their way of life is sustainable shilling and HODLing for life, creating decades of generational wealth.

On weekend benders, when they’ve had one-too-many shots of their favourite selection of freshly squeezed snake venom, the HoneyBadgers indulge themselves by foraging for those lost lost relics, the invaluable ancient NFT artefacts.

So why leave this little utopia that they have created, in the search for something completely unknown? As any modern civilisation when faced with an existential uncertainty would do, the Honeybadgers set upon their journey to find the truth.

But what challenges and horrors are they to find along this path?

It is time to introduce these 10,000 truly non-fungible creatures. Immune to the venom of snakes and society, these HoneyBadgers are now on a quest to find the truth behind what really happened at Base 721.

The legend of these HoneyBadgers mirror the journey of every crypto HODLer and believer. These NFTs immortalise the resilience, the tenacity and the strength of these HoneyBadgers. They are a monument to the characteristics of every player, big and small, in the cryptoverse; those who stand strong together in the face of adversity, with an unrelenting belief in changing the fundamentals of the system. Sometimes, the only way forward is to just not care.

What is an NFT?

NFTs stand for "Non-fungible tokens", but this probably sounds more confusing than clarifying. Simply explained, an NFT can be thought of as a unique digital asset with blockchain-based ownership that users can buy, own, and trade. While the leading use cases of NFTs have been seen in the ‘tokenization’ of digital art, this is only but a glimpse into the disruptive potential of NFTs in the digital era of human civilisation. NFTs can be engineered through smart-contracts to the limitations only of our imagination (and programming skills) - think exclusive access to websites, event tickets, game items, ownership records for physical objects, intellectual property… This list can go on.

To sum things up, not only does your NFT serve as a ‘certificate of authenticity’, it also acts as your ‘members-only’ access card to the most exclusive of clubs. The NF Honey Badgers club is as exclusive as it gets.

What is a Metamask wallet and how to use it?

Metamask is a crypto-wallet (available both as a web browser plugin/extension as well as a mobile app) that can store your Ethereum and all Ethereum based tokens (including NFTs). You will need to create a Metamask account if you do not already have one, as this will be needed to purchase and mint an NF Honey Badger. Once you have an account, you shall be able to view your unique Ethereum address - this is where your NFT will be stored upon minting. If you are making a new Metamask account, remember to securely note down your ‘SecretRecoveryPhrase’. Learn more about Metamask and how easy it is to use over here!(https://metamask.io)

+
New to crypto?

Once you have your Metamask wallet set up either as a browser extension (currently supports Chrome, Firefox, Brave and Edge) or through its app on your phone (Android/iOS), you will need to top it up with some Ethereum in order to mint an NF Honey Badger. If you need to first purchase some Ethereum in order to transfer it to your Metamask wallet, you can use any reputable cryptocurrency exchange platform whose services are available in your jurisdiction. A few popular examples of such exchanges are Coinbase, Binance, Kraken, KuCoin, FTX etc. Make sure that you transfer some surplus Ethereum into your Metamask wallet over and above the amount you require for minting (0.05 ETH per NFT), as you will be charged some ‘gas fees’ by the Ethereum network for minting your NFT(s). The exact gas fees that may be levied would depend on the network congestion at the time, but have a little extra Ethereum in there just to be on the safe side so that you secure your minting.

How do I mint an NF Honeybadger NFT on my laptop/computer?

If you are using a desktop or a laptop to mint our NF Honey Badgers, all you need to do is connect your web browser’s Metamask plugin/extension with our website by clicking on the ‘connect to metamask’ button on the top right hand side. Once our sale is open, you shall be able to easily select how many Honey Badgers you desire to mint and simply click on the ‘Mint’ button which shall be made readily visible on the website. If you have enough ETH to complete your desired transaction, your minting transaction should go through in no time and your brand new NF Honey Badger NFT shall be sent to your Metamask wallet address associated with your account. You will be able to see your NFT, visually, on Opensea once you connect your Metamask account to it

What if I am using a phone?

You can mint an NF Honeybadger by downloading Metamask’s Android/iOS application and accessing the website through its built-in browser. After installation, launch the Metamask app and login/sign up. Click on the menu bar (the 3 lines on the top left of your screen) and select "Browser". When you access www.nfhoneybadgers.com via this browser, you can click on the ‘Connect to Metamask’ button in order to connect your wallet and to start minting!
